Welcome to Pin-Board, the minimalist, always-on-top workspace built for focus and speed.

## Installation

1.  **Download**: Head over to our [Download Page](https://pinboard.pro/download) and grab the binary for your OS (Windows, macOS, or Linux).
2.  **Run**: Pin-Board is a portable native app. Just run the executable, and you're ready to go. No installation wizards required.
3.  **Pin it**: By default, Pin-Board stays on top of all other windows.

## Core Features

-   **Native Rust Engine**: Zero-latency performance.
-   **Always on Top**: Your notes, timers, and ideas never get lost behind other windows.
-   **Minimalist UI**: Distraction-free design that blends into your workflow.

## First Steps

-   **Create a Note**: Just start typing in a new pin.
-   **Timer**: Set a quick countdown to stay focused on your current task.
-   **Transparency**: Hover over a pin and use the scroll wheel or slider to adjust opacity.

Enjoy your new, focused workspace!
